Twitchy

  • High Concept: Fast-paced keep-away!
  • Player Experience Goals: Anxiety/strong focus to keep the object from hitting the balls.
  • Genre: Twitch games.
  • Game play: Move the mouse pointer around and the screen to keep the orange object away from the blue balls. Click to start/restart.
  • Prototype goal: Having states for starting, winning, losing, or restarting the game. Also have improved random movement since last game. The objective is a blend between Chase and Escape, which is different from both of my previous games which were Capture.
  • Features: Very colorful, making it difficult to see where everything is.
  • Setting/Premise: NA
  • Story: NA
  • Target Audience: People who like twitch games.
  • Play time: 32 seconds
  • Strategies: Keeping the mouse close to the object.
